Frequently Asked Questions
What does "unlimited designs" mean?
Our unlimited design subscription offers you the freedom to request as many design projects as you need, covering any number of brands. We work on them one at a time, ensuring each receives the dedicated attention it deserves, with a turnaround of up to 3 business days per request.
How does the design request process work?
We invite you to discuss your project with us. Just email! Share your requirements, provide any essential files, and based on the project's scope, our dedicated team will ensure personalized attention in regards to your membership delivery timeline. We're committed to addressing each request with the care and the priority it deserves.
What kind of design tasks can I request?
You can request a wide range of design tasks, including application interfaces, digital marketing materials, mobile app designs, complete web design projects, and more. You have the freedom to make unlimited requests. Unsure if your project falls within our expertise? Feel free to inquire!
Can I really cancel anytime?
Absolutely. Our subscription model is designed to be flexible, understanding that your design needs might fluctuate. You can cancel your subscription at any moment, no questions asked.
What if I need revisions?
No design is finalized until you're completely satisfied. Our unlimited design service includes unlimited revisions, ensuring that the final deliverable meets your expectations perfectly.
What is the typical delivery time for design projects?
Delivery times vary based on the complexity of the design request. Simple tasks like social media graphics are often completed within 1 business day, while more detailed projects, such as full-page designs, will take no more than 3 business days for the initial draft.
Who will be working on my designs?
At Dapp Designers, your projects are in the hands of our pros. Our team's expertise spans a variety of design fields, ensuring top-tier results for every project.
What if I only have a single design need?
Our service is built to accommodate both ongoing and one-off design needs. If you only have a single request, you can simply pause your subscription once your current project is completed and reactivate it whenever you're ready for more. Have a specific project? Let’s talk!
Is there any discount for long-term commitments?
Yes, we offer a significant discount for clients who commit to an annual subscription. This option provides great savings while securing a continuous, high-quality design service for all your projects.
How do I track the progress of my design requests?
Depending on your membership, you'll receive personalized updates and can provide feedback directly via email, ensuring seamless communication with our expert designers.
